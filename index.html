INTERACTIONS WITH NOTES

1. Click LMB on the panel to create a note.

2. Click LMB on a note to select it, click SHIFT+LMB to select several notes. Click ESCAPE to remove selection.

3. Click RMB on a note to delete it. Click DELETE to delete selected note(-s).

4. You can drag or resize selected note(-s) using LMB.

5. When note(-s) is(are) selected, you can set the number of cents (use "0-9", ".", BACKSPACE, then hit ENTER)

6. Use Ctrl+A to select all notes.

7. Press ArrowUp and ArrowDown to shift note(-s) up or down by a cent.

7. Press SHIFT+ArrowUp and SHIFT+ArrowDown to shift note(-s) up or down by an octave.

OTHER

1. Use Ctrl+Scroll to zoom in/out.

2. Use Spacebar to play/stop.

3. Press "Z", "X", "C", ..., "A", "S", "D", ..., "Q", "W", "E", ... buttons while in "Define keys" mode to play notes.
